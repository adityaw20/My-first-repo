Koenigsegg jesko

The Koenigsegg Jesko is motivated by a twin-turbocharged 5.0-liter V-8
generating 1280 horsepower and 1106 lb-ft of torque on pump gas.
On E85, output jumps to 1600 horsepower.
Koenigsegg claims that the engine features the world's lightest V-8 crankshaft.

Price:-Starts from 21crore
