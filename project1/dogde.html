Dodge Challenger SRT Hellcat



The Dodge Challenger SRT Hellcat is a high-performance muscle car with a 6.2L
supercharged HEMI V8 engine. It produces 707 horsepower and 650 lb-ft of torque.
The Hellcat can go from 0 to 60 miles per hour in 3.6 seconds and has a top speed of
about 202 miles per hour.
